    Gujarat

    Gujarat is a vibrant state known for its rich cultural heritage, historical significance, and thriving economy. It's epitomizes a harmonious blend of tradition and modernity, making it a captivating destination.

    • Delhi

    Delhi, the vibrant capital city of India.Beyond its tangible charms, Delhi pulsates with energy, serving as a melting pot of diverse cultures, cuisines, and traditions, making it a truly unforgettable destination.

    • Mumbai

    Mumbai, the vibrant metropolis on India's western coast, pulsates with energy and diversity. As the financial, commercial, and entertainment capital of India, it is a city of contrasts. It's also a bollwood city.

    • Uttar Pradesh

    Uttar Pradesh is a state in northern India. With over 241 million inhabitants, it is the most populated state in India as well as the most populous country subdivision in the world. The state is divided into 18 divisions.

    • Kerala

    Kerala, a state on India's tropical Malabar Coast, has nearly 600km of Arabian Sea shoreline. It's known for its palm-lined beaches and backwaters, a network of canals. Support tea, coffee, spice plantations as well as wildlife.

    • Rajasthan

    Rajasthan is a state in northern India. It covers 342,239 square kilometres or 10.4 per cent of India's total geographical area. It is the largest Indian state by area and the seventh largest by population.
